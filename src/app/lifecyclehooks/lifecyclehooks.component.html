Angular Lifecycle Hooks

  1. 1️⃣ ngOnChanges() – Called when an Input() property changes.
  2. 2️⃣ ngOnInit() – Called once after the first ngOnChanges().
  3. 3️⃣ ngDoCheck() – Custom change detection.
  4. 4️⃣ ngAfterContentInit() – After content (ng-content) has been projected into view.
  5. 5️⃣ ngAfterContentChecked() – After every check of projected content.
  6. 6️⃣ ngAfterViewInit() – After component's views and child views are initialized.
  7. 7️⃣ ngAfterViewChecked() – After every check of component's views and child views.
  8. 8️⃣ ngOnDestroy() – Just before the component is destroyed.
